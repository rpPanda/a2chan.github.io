We offer content for
Blog Posts
Blog posts are generally 500-700 word long content pieces which are usually one of the most published forms of content that each organisation associates with its product. With the right length to keep the readers engaged along with providing the requisite information in a concise and crisp manner, blog posts get the maximum of internet traffic depending on the use of specific key words.
Web Pages
Webpages need to be keyword rich and should provide the reader, the requisite information and resources that the particular website provides for. Usually 800-1000 words long, SEO optimisation is a key tool involved. Website content can be something as commonplace and straightforward as your company's "About Us" text, it can also be pages that are regularly added to and updated.
Social Media Content
Social media content provides for special curated posts which align engagement, virality, retweets, shares, followers and all the other good things that come to social media marketers. With SMO integration, social media posts are usually 100-200 words and work best when regularly updated on premium social media platforms.
Articles
Articles are detailed content pieces that feature in-depth research and statistical driven conclusions. They are among the most potential lead-creators which allow clients to gather as much information as possible in a short time frame.
Whitepapers
Whitepapers are industry based content pieces, that are legislative documents which usually describe a theory behind a particular technology or an industry. They are 2000-2200 in word size and are persuasive, authoritative and provide an in-depth report on a specific topic that presents a problem and provides a solution.
Press releases
Press releases are formal pieces of content that are used to deliver informational updates about your organisation to media professionals. With an added incentive of better coverage as compared to generic advertisement pieces. They are usually 400-500 words and provide for recorded communication directed at members of the news media for declarations.
Product description
Product description is a structured format of presenting information about a product which includes its benefits, form, presentation, price, purpose etc. Successful sellers, whether in a store or online, know the importance of a great product description. Besides the visually appeal of the product, it is the product description that assists in selling the product.
Compnay profile
A company profile is a concise description of a firm which usually include its mission statement, history, values, its human and financial resources, along with its goods and services among other things. The company profile, provides an overall idea about what the company is. They are usually 800-1000 word long and informative content pieces.
Academic writing
Academic Writing is usually conducted in several sets of forms and genres and is usually targeted towards a critical and informed audience. Intended to reinforce or challenge concepts or arguments, they are usually 8-10 pages long and are adequately detailed. Formal academic rheotoric often finds use in this type of writing.
